About Us

“ANDARBA” which in Hindi/Gujarati means ‘together, co-operation, a collective or support’, founded in 2016, is an NGO in India registered as a Public Charitable Trust and is located in MALPUR, Gujarat.


“ANDARBA” closely works with the deprived communities and empowers the socially marginalized by providing them livelihood services, and imparting skills leading to employment, health and education facilities. “ANDARBA” focuses on livelihood services, child rights, human rights, young girls’ education, skill development and other community services.
